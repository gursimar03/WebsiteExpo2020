Introduction

We all have the ability to make the world a better place and to impact the future. Discover how we're 'Connecting Minds and Creating the Future' through sustainability, mobility, and opportunity at Expo 2020 Dubai. Expo 2020 Dubai will run from October 1, 2021, until March 31, 2022. Expo 2020 Dubai will be the first World Expo to be held in the MEASA region (Middle East, Africa, and South Asia).

It is one of the first truly worldwide, post-epidemic international gatherings of states, having been postponed for a year due to the Covid-19 pandemic. 'Connecting Minds, Creating the Future' is the theme of Expo 2020 Dubai, with three sub-themes of sustainability, mobility, and opportunity. This theme promises to make this a World Expo that inspires people by displaying the best instances of global collaboration, innovation, and cooperation.

AI And Technology

In October 2017, the UAE Government launched ‘UAE Strategy for Artificial Intelligence (AI)’. This marks the post-mobile government phase which will rely on various future services, sectors and infrastructure projects. The strategy is first of its kind in the region and the world and it aims to:

  • Achieve the objectives of UAE Centennial 2071
  • Boost government performance at all levels
  • Use an integrated smart digital system that can overcome challenges and provide quick efficient solutions
  • Make the UAE the first in the field of AI investments in various sectors
  • Create new vital market with high economic value.


Ireland In Dubai EXPO 2020

Ireland has been a source of creativity and innovation for 5,000 years and is known as the "Island of Inspiration." The subject for Ireland is based on our history and culture, but it also addresses a critical challenge for humanity in the coming age of artificial intelligence and robotics. Empathy, human connection, and community are all enhanced through creativity. Non-confrontational responses to diversity are facilitated through creative expression, which also serves as a forum for addressing complicated personal and societal issues. This is the point we're making at Expo Dubai: the importance of putting creativity at the center of the 21st century human experience. Our topic is in line with Expo 2020's general theme of "Connecting Minds, Creating the Future." Over the six months of Expo programming, our goal is to bring this idea into debate and discussion, and to position the topic of human creativity in terms of humanity's future well-being.

Main Focus : Team Ireland

Government Departments and State Agencies work collaboratively with other Irish Stakeholders to boost our international reputation and drive the development of trade, tourism, investment, science, technology, and innovation, culture, and education through a Team Ireland strategy. Every day, Team Ireland overseas, with the help of support teams at home, works to find and seize new opportunities for Ireland. Team Ireland will use Expo 2020 as a major platform. To ensure an impactful Ireland presence, the Department of Foreign Affairs collaborates with government departments, state agencies, business, arts and culture, academia, and the creative industries.

Food & Drinks

Bord Bia is working with Ireland’s Pavilion team to showcase the very best of Irish food and drink throughout the 6 months of Expo, including events that will gather top chiefs, hotel and restaurant owners and influencers. The Food, Agriculture and Livelihoods theme week at Expo will be a particular focus with the leading Gulfood trade show arranged to coincide. Representatives of Irish food and agriculture will have significant speaking platforms during this time.

Tourism

Visitors will be able to ‘Press the Green Button’ with Tourism Ireland throughout the 6 months at Ireland’s Pavilion and learn more about Ireland’s sights, culture and people. There will be a number of tourism focused events and communications initiatives throughout Expo. The Travel and Connectivity theme week at Expo will provide an additional focus on Ireland as a travel destination.
